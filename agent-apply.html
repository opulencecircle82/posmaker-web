🎯 Welcome to the Affiliate Program

As a POSMaker affiliate, your job is to help business owners set up their POS with POSMaker — you get your own personal referral link to share with them.
  • Once approved, your referral link becomes active and you can start sharing it with business owners.
  • When someone signs up using your link, it shows up in your Affiliate Dashboard so you can track every store you've referred — and in our admin dashboard for review.
  • You earn a recurring monthly commission (e.g. 10%) on the subscription paid by every store you referred — for as long as they stay subscribed. Your exact rate is shown in your dashboard once approved.
  • Free months can also be granted to the stores you refer as part of their onboarding.
  • Your application is reviewed by our team — this usually takes a few days.

Terms & Conditions

By applying to the POSMaker Affiliate Program, you agree to the following:

1. You will represent POSMaker honestly and accurately when referring business owners.

2. Your application will be reviewed by the POSMaker team, and acceptance is at our sole discretion.

3. Your referral link is personal to you and may not be shared in a misleading or spam-like manner.

4. Free-month promotions granted through your referral link apply to the stores you refer, not to your own account.

5. You earn a monthly commission on the active subscription of each store you referred, at the rate shown in your affiliate dashboard, for as long as that store remains a paying subscriber.

6. POSMaker may suspend or revoke your affiliate status at any time for violation of these terms.

7. Commission payouts, schedules, and methods will be communicated to you separately by the POSMaker team.
