Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?+
You are charged monthly starting from the date you subscribe. You can cancel at any time and your access continues until the end of the billing period.
Can I use POSMaker on multiple devices?+
Yes! POSMaker works on any device with a browser — Android phone, tablet, or Windows PC. The Add POS plan allows multiple cashier accounts across devices.
Do I need internet to use POSMaker?+
An internet connection is required for real-time sync and reports. Basic POS functions may still work briefly if connection drops temporarily.
How do I get my store set up?+
After subscribing, contact our support and we'll set up your store account, add your products, and get you started — usually within 24 hours.
